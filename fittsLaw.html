Fitts's Law Demo

Fitts's Law predicts how long it takes to point to a target based on how far away it is and how large it is. Click the orange target from the center start zone as quickly and accurately as you can.

The demo uses different distance and target size combinations. After each click, the program records the time and shows your movement time alongside the Fitts' law index of difficulty.
